02

About

I've spent my whole research life circling one question: how do people actually work? I've chased it through the body, the brain, the social worlds people build around themselves, and now into what happens as AI integrates into every part of that world. The field kept changing; the question never did.

These days, that means studying human–AI interactions directly. I conducted longitudinal and cross-sectional studies to explore how sustained use of AI and social chatbots affects people's wellbeing and real relationships. In industry, I've done the same kind of work on AI products people use every day, turning messy behavioral and telemetry data into findings that teams can act on.
