## In brief

Experimental social-psychology research examining how structured intergroup contact shifts attitudes between groups — designing the manipulation, measuring change over time, and modeling the effects while accounting for individual differences.

## What this project demonstrates

- Designing controlled experiments to test causal hypotheses
- Building valid measures of attitudes and behavior
- Analyzing nested data with mixed-effects models
- Translating theory into testable, well-powered designs

**Methods & tools:** experimental design · mixed-effects models · psychometrics · R
