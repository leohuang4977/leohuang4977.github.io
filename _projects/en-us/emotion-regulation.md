## In brief

A lab study of how different emotion-regulation strategies change the way people weigh risk and reward — pairing a behavioral choice task with computational modeling to separate what shifts in the decision process itself.

## What this project demonstrates

- Designing lab experiments that isolate a mechanism
- Pairing behavioral tasks with computational models of choice
- Connecting affective science to applied decision-making
- Communicating modeling results to non-technical audiences
