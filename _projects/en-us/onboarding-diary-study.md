## In brief

A longitudinal diary study following a cohort of new users through their first week with a product — capturing in-the-moment friction, expectations, and drop-off points, then synthesizing them into a journey map the product team could prioritize against.

## What this project demonstrates

- Designing and running longitudinal, in-context research
- Turning scattered diary entries into a structured journey map
- Connecting qualitative friction points to measurable drop-off
- Prioritizing findings into an actionable backlog

**Methods & tools:** diary study · journey mapping · thematic analysis · longitudinal research
